Platemate Mobile App
×

 PlateMate allows the users to enter their body makeup with the goal bodyweight they want to achieve. They can keep track of their daily calorie amount by adding foods to each meal and portioning each item to within their calorie allotment. Users can create foods, edit meals, and reset their calories once the new day begins. All data is stored, persisted to be usable offline, and passed easily using React Redux.
